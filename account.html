Viewing Order History

Log in and go to "My Orders". All your past and current purchases are neatly listed with details like order date, items, and delivery status.

Cancel Orders

You can cancel your order as long as it hasn’t been delivered. Simply go to the Main Menu → Cancel → select the item → provide a reason → confirm.

Time Limit for Cancellation

Orders can typically be cancelled before they are processed for delivery. Once shipped, cancellation is not possible.

Partial Cancellation

If your order contains multiple items, you can cancel individual items before they are shipped.
