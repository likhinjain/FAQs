Cancel Shipped Orders

Once your order has shipped, cancellation is no longer possible. However, you can initiate a return after delivery. Our support team will guide you through the process to ensure you get a refund or replacement quickly.

Cancel Before Delivery

If your order hasn’t been delivered yet, you can cancel it easily. Go to the Main Menu, select “Cancel,” choose the item, provide a reason, and confirm. The cancellation will be processed instantly.

Modify Orders

Need to change your order? Contact support immediately. Modifications are only possible before shipping. Once shipped, you’ll need to initiate a return or exchange.

Wrong Item Ordered

If you ordered the wrong item, don’t worry. You can cancel before shipping or return/exchange after delivery. Our team will help you get the right product.

Adding Items

You cannot add items to an existing order. Place a new order instead. We’re working on bundled checkout options to make this easier in the future.

Cancellation Charges

There are no cancellation fees if done before shipping. After shipping, return policies apply. We aim to keep the process fair and transparent.
